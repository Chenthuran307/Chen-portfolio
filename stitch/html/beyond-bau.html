Productivity AI Figma Plugin

Comment Analyzer Plugin

The Problem

Figma comment threads lack organization and sentiment categorization, creating a manual triage bottleneck during team reviews.

What was built

An LLM-powered Figma plugin that automatically aggregates and tags comments by category (e.g. UX bug, copy suggestion) and priority.

Outcome

Reduced critique triage and action-item review times by 40%.

Team Personality Workshop

The Problem

What was done

AI Agent Design Systems Accessibility

Design System Assistant Agent

The Problem

Designers and developers waste time cross-referencing design tokens, content rules, and accessibility docs, leading to handoff issues.

What was built

An AI copilot equipped with component, content, and accessibility specs. Supports screen uploads to detect design issues, handles general design doubts, and answers developer queries on pattern specs.

Outcome

Tested it with 10 designers, saving 30% of their time and allowing them to focus on user problems instead of findability issues.
