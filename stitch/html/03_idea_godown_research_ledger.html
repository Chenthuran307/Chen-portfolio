Home Inventory Intelligence

Observation

The average modern household manages more SKUs than a small 1950s convenience store, yet uses zero inventory management systems beyond 'looking in the pantry'.

Research

Interviewed 12 households; 100% reported 'double-buying' or 'running out' of staples monthly. Cognitive load of 'remembering what we have' is a primary stressor in meal planning.

Problem Statement

"How might we apply industrial supply-chain thinking to the domestic sphere to eliminate the 'pantry-check' cognitive loop?"

The Idea

A headless tracking system using receipt-parsing AI and weight-sensitive shelving. Not a 'smart fridge', but a 'smart infrastructure'.

Prototype Vibe

Low-fi dashboard focusing on 'burn rates' rather than list items. Dashboard looks like an ERP system but for milk, eggs, and detergent.

Pulse Adaptive Gaming

Observation

Difficulty settings are static, yet player stress and flow states are dynamic. A 'Hard' mode when you're tired is different from 'Hard' when you're peaking.

Research

Wearable HR data is readily available via APIs but rarely piped into the game loop for difficulty adjustment.

Problem Statement

"Can we maintain a perfect 'Flow' state by adjusting game intensity based on real-time biometric feedback?"

The Idea

A middleware plugin for Unity/Unreal that modulates NPC aggression and puzzle complexity based on Heart Rate Variability (HRV).

Prototype Vibe

A horror game demo where the monster only spawns when your heart rate drops below a certain 'calm' threshold.

Idea 03
Product Strategy Medium (Niche creator templates)
Status
Experimenting

AI-Powered Creative Intelligence Platform for Fitness Creators

Observation

Fitness creators spend more time researching trends, styles, and audio than actually filming their workouts.

Research

Existing tools focus on editing mechanics or generic viral trends, but fail to answer: "What format works for *my* specific video?"

Problem Statement

"There is no intelligent system that can analyze raw workout footage and recommend personalized, niche-specific creative templates."

The Idea

An AI Creative Director that analyzes uploaded footage, identifies the exercise, matches it with fitness trends, and outputs editable reel templates.

Prototype Vibe

A streamlined dashboard where you upload a raw squat video and instantly receive 3 high-converting storytelling frameworks (e.g. Anime Power-Up, Rep Counter).

Idea 04
Product Strategy Medium (Luxury market convergence)
Status
Concept Stage

AirPods Earrings

Observation

Hearables are transitioning from 'gadgets' to 'body-worn essentials'. Current designs are still stuck in a 'tech-first' industrial aesthetic.

Research

Growing market for jewelry that 'wraps' or 'holds' existing AirPods to prevent loss and add style.

Problem Statement

"Why can't the hearable itself be the jewelry, rather than an accessory to it?"

The Idea

Modular ear-worn devices where the 'tech core' snaps into high-fashion earring chassis.

Prototype Vibe

Renders exploring the intersection of silver-work and driver housing. 'Fashion-tech convergence'.

Idea 06
Product Strategy High (Narrative reasoning space)
Status
Exploration

Intelligent Story Companion

Observation

Creators know what they want to say but struggle with how to make people feel it. Existing AI writes for them, but doesn't explain why a story works.

Research

There is no unified platform that combines storytelling theory, psychology, and emotional analysis into a single intelligent assistant.

Problem Statement

"Story creators don't need another AI that writes for them—they need an AI that understands stories and diagnoses structural weaknesses."

The Idea

An AI-powered story architect that analyzes, understands, and improves stories instead of simply generating them.

Prototype Vibe

A dashboard where users ask "Why doesn't this scene work?" and receive diagnostics on pacing, emotional payoff, and character arcs.

Idea 07
Product Strategy High (Organizational memory)
Status
Active Pursuit

AI Meeting Intelligence

Observation

Every meeting generates valuable knowledge, yet once the meeting ends, that intelligence is lost or scattered. The gap is not in capturing meetings, but in learning from them.

Research

Most solutions focus on recording and notes, leaving a major gap in explaining decision rationale, cross-meeting connections, and team collaboration dynamics.

Problem Statement

"Organizations don't need another meeting recorder—they need a system that understands conversations, preserves context, and tracks decision rationale."

The Idea

A platform that transforms every meeting into a living organizational knowledge graph, linking decisions, projects, and people over time.

Prototype Vibe

An interactive dashboard showing project decision timelines, participation stats, question quality scores, and personal communication coaching.

Idea 08
Product Strategy Medium (Enterprise knowledge tools)
Status
Archived / Validated

Design Knowledge Agent

Observation

Designers waste hours searching for company-specific UI guidelines and previous research findings.

Research

Internal audits show 30% rework due to 'siloed knowledge'.

Why I Still Include It

"While this has been largely 'solved' by new enterprise tools (and thus archived as a unique product idea), the core problem of 'Organizational Amnesia' remains a fascination of mine."

The Idea

An LLM trained exclusively on a company's past Figma files, Jira tickets, and research decks.

Market Status

Market Validated. Several startups now offering this. Idea archived to focus on more blue-ocean hypotheses.
